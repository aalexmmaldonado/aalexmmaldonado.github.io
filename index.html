Experience

Keith Lab

University of Pittsburgh

Modeling solvation effects in computational chemistry is not always straight forward. Continuum solvent models are sometimes unpredictable and molecular dynamics at ab initio quality are prohibitively expensive. We develop and apply various solvent treatments and methodologies to model solvated reactions and gather physically meaningful insights.

Young Lab

Western Michigan University

Point-of-care tests (POCT) are revolutionizing healthcare by bringing advanced medical diagnostics to patients' bedside. Current POCT, such as Abbott’s i-STAT, are still extremely complex and fairly expensive. The Young Lab focuses on developing a novel immunodiagnostics device aimed at expanding POCT possibilities.
